About

I am a retired amiture hobbyist photographer who enjoys bringing my camers for a walk. Photography offers a unique escape, a chance to disconnect from chaos and reconecct with your inner self. It’s a mindful practice that encourages you to be fully present in the moment. It encourages taking a slower, more observant approach to life, as you frame a shot, adjusting your focus and composing the elements just right, the rest of the world fades into the background. Your attention shifts to the details, the interplay of light and shadow, and the subtle nuances that make each scene come alive. This for me is the enjoyment I get from photography. I hop you enjoy these moments as well...
